Flight Bulter is a travel mobile app that provides one-stop travel services including flight delay inquiries, airfare, hotel reservations, mobile check-in, airport information navigation and more.

Based on the business travel scene, the flight butler also made several extended scene products, including the “Huoli Hotel” (provide hotel reservation and recommendation service), “Huoli Cars” (provide drop off and pick up service), “Huoli Selection” (online Shopp), and strive to provide a closed-loop travel experience for users.

Membership Card redesign

Under the trend of consumption upgrading, people are not only satisfied with the most basic needs of food, clothing, housing and transportation, but also prefer the quality and personalized travel consumption concept, and would like to obtain a perfect travel service experience. The Flight Bulter witch has been deep-diving travel service field, with a large number of users traveling data, continues to pay close attention to the personalized travel needs of various travel groups, and digging into diversified travel scene services, thereby launching a membership growth system, aiming at offering a more refined and customized one-stop travel service experience.

As a product manager of Huoli ( Huoli and Flight Bulter are brother products in the same company.), I was invited to join in the whole membership card redesign.

The idea behind was great, but had some usability issues. I did a thorough evaluation on the membership card and provided design suggestions. Some selected highlights and recommendations:

The color tone is too calm

Even if most users are business travellers, the color scheme is still too calm, lacking of a lively and active atmosphere.

The card style lacks texture

It’s hard to notice the specialities of being a member from the card design. I suggesed to design a unique logo for each level of membership and improviethe texture of the card.

The level rights are not clear

The rights of members are not intuitive enough which has greatly reduced the attractiveness. I recommended listing all membership rights so that users can have a complete understanding of the whole system. Meanwhile using illustrations to describ scenes and rights will help users understand.

Redesigned membership card system

The suggestions I gave to Flight Bulter design team led great design improvements for the current membership system design

Membership rights display

Show all rights directly and highlights the ones alreay have. This imply users to upgrade membership to unlock the other rights.
